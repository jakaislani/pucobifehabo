Who Is Eula Valdez Son? What We Know About Miguel Valdez

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, September 9, 2024

Miguel Valdez is the adoring son of Eula Valdez, a legendary Filipina actress. Scroll down if you want to learn more about Miguel.

Eula is most known for playing Amor Powers in the original Pangako Sa ‘Yo (2000) and Janice in the Bagets film series (1984).

She portrayed Corazon de Leon, a devoted wife and mother, in the 2019 film The General’s Daughter.

Then, in 2020, she portrayed “Lucy Wong,” a harsh and overprotective mother and wife in Love Thy Woman. In Huwag Kang Mangamba, she plays Deborah delos Santos, a religious zealot turned faith healer, in 2021.

Who Is Eula Valdez Son Miguel Valdez?

Eula Valdez’s son’s name is Miguel Valdez. The son and his mother have a close relationship, and Eula frequently posts images of them on her Instagram account.

Apart from that, Miguel, the eldest, is an artist who has worked with Altum Animus and Edge.

Furthermore, he is already married and has his own life to deal with. He married on October 22, 2021, according to his Instagram posts.

His mother went returned to ABS-CBN in 2017. In the T.V. series The Good Son, she played “Olivia Buenavidez,” Victor Buenavidez’s manipulative and deceitful wife.

Miguel Valdez Age: How Old Is He?

Miguel Valdez, Eula Valdez’s son, is 27 years old. He has yet to reveal his date of birth on the Internet.

His mother, Eula, is 53 years and was born on December 11, 1968.

Eula earned a Bachelor of Arts in Asian Studies and was a varsity volleyball player at the University of Santo Tomas Faculty of Arts and Letters.

Who Are Miguel Valdez Parents?

Miguel Valdez parents are Eula Valdez and Rocky Salumbides.

On August 30, 1980, Rocky Salumbides was born. Rocky’s birth name is Alvin Kirby Liwanag Salumbides.

He is an actor who has appeared in films such as Lily (2016), Tarima (2010), and Blindfold (2015).

Eula starred in the T.V. series Maria Flordeluna in 2007. After the series ended, she did a follow-up series on ABS-rival CBN’s network, G.M.A. Network, named Kamandag, which featured Richard Gutierrez.

Since then, she has starred in several G.M.A. Network series, including All About Eve, the short-lived Saturday paranormal series E.S.P., and LaLola.

She returned to ABS-CBN in 2009 to star in the primetime smash series The Wedding, followed by The Last Prince and the afternoon serial Koreana in 2010.
