James Murray Biography and Wiki

James Murray is an American improvisational comedian, actor, author, and producer born and brought up in Staten Island, New York, United States. Murray is well known for his starring role in the television series, Impractical Jokers. Additionally, James is also known as a member of the Tenderloins, a comedy troupe that consists of Sal Vulcano, Joe Gatto, and Brian Quinn.

James Murray Age: How Old Is He?

James Stephen ‘Murr’ Murray was born on May 1st, 1976, in Staten Island, New York, United States. He celebrates his birthday every May 1st of every year. Murray will be 45 years old on May 1st, 2021. His birth sign is Taurus.

James Murray Height

Murray stands at a height of 6ft 0.5inches(1.80m). Also, James weighs 70kgs(154lbs).

James Murray Education

Murray is a graduate of Monsignor Farrell High School. He went to high school together with Sal Vulcano, Joe Gatto, and Brian Quinn. The four of them were members of the school’s Improvisation Club. Later on, they separated and James joined Georgetown University. Murray holds a bachelor’s degree in English.

James Murray Wife, Is James Murray married?

Murray was married to Jenna Vulcano in 2014. This was as a punishment for Sal in the season 3 finale, ‘Brother-in-Loss’. However, they annulled their marriage on the next day after their marriage.

James Murray Girlfriend

On August 30, 2019, Murr proposed to Melyssa Davies in New Jersey. Their proposal was initially a picnic and champagne out. However, Murray had planned to propose when they were on vacation in Turks and Caicos during the Labor Day weekend. The initial plan was canceled due to Hurricane Dorian in the Bahamas on Sunday.

Murray and Davies met by chance after attending a launch party for Murr’s novel, Awakened. Both of them have one Instagram account “murrandmelyssa” and its bio reads:

James Murray Girlfriend: Melyssa Davies, Who is Melyssa Davies?

James Murray Comedian

Murray began by producing, writing, and directing the film, Damned, in 1998. ‘Damned’ stars Jeremy Guskin as Jesus and it retells the Bible. Murr reunited with Sal, Joe, and Brian after graduating from the different colleges. Sal, James, Brian, and Joe began practicing improvisation at Joe’s house. In 1999, they traveled on tours as an improv and sketch comedy troupe, The Tenderloins.

Later on, they began sharing their comedy sketched on YouTube, MySpace, and Metacafe. The comedy sketches earned millions of views and they won a $100,000 grand prize in the NBC Competition Show, It’s Your Show’ with the Time Thugs Sketch.

James Murray Impractical Jokers

The show, Impractical Jokers, first premiered on December 15th, 2011, on TruTV. Its first season accumulated over 32 million views and thus it ended becoming among the most popular series on TruTv. Additionally, Murray and the other members of the Tenderloins were cast in the Mistery Index, hosted by Jameela Jamil. The movie Impractical Jokers was released on February 21st, 2020. In addition to that, Murr and the Tenderloins host their podcast since April 2012.
