Where Is Suspect Jamere Miller?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

This article will include information about Bianca Green missing case and the suspect, Jamere Miller. 

Jamere Miller was arrested on Monday concerning the murder of 29-year-old Patrice Wilson. Wilson, a nurse from Detroit, was allegedly in a relationship with Miller.

She was kidnapped from Detroit Receiving Hospital after finishing her shift and was subsequently discovered deceased in the trunk of her vehicle.

This recent arrest has also brought attention to another missing woman’s case from over a decade ago, which Miller is believed to be connected to.

Bianca Green Missing Update 2023

Bianca Chanel Green, also called “Be Be,” disappeared on March 25, 2011, at 24. At the time of her disappearance, Green was a mother and has not had any contact with her family or close associates since then.

Authorities have indicated that she may have been facing suicidal thoughts and was unlikely to leave voluntarily for an extended period.

When she was last seen, she wore her work uniform: black pants and a white shirt. Her shoe size is six. Additionally, she had earrings and a silver chain necklace with a diamond heart pendant.

Green has a mole on her nose and two tattoos regarding identifying features. One tattoo on her lower back depicts a heart with a design inside. The other tattoo on her right wrist shows a sunflower wearing a crown.

The family of Bianca Green is still desperately searching for answers seven years after her mysterious disappearance. 

Lisa Green, her mother, expressed the immense length of time they have gone without any news of her daughter, causing them great anguish.

The unresolved case has been brought to public attention by Crime Stoppers for the second time.

Lisa Green emphasized the profound impact of losing a child, comparing it to losing a button on a shirt, but on a much grander scale.

The concerned family, including a caring uncle and Bianca’s mother, are urging the local community to take action.

Where Is Suspect Jamere Miller Now?

Detroit Police Chief James White has announced the arrest of a suspect believed to be responsible for the kidnapping and murder of a Detroit nurse.

The suspect, 37 -year-old Jamere Miller, surrendered to the police after the execution of several search warrants. The arrest took place on Sunday afternoon.

Chief White described Miller as an individual with a violent nature, citing his extensive criminal record and a history of domestic violence.

The arrest of Miller provides a significant development in the ongoing investigation into the case.

According to the police, Patrice Wilson was abducted on Saturday while entering her vehicle at Detroit Receiving Hospital.

The following day, her lifeless body was discovered inside her car, parked outside an apartment complex in Novi.

On Monday, the police confirmed that the suspect, Jamere Miller, had a relationship with Bianca Green, a pregnant woman who vanished over ten years ago. This connection adds another layer of complexity to the case.

While relieved that the suspect had been apprehended, Wilson’s grandmother, Pamela Golden, expressed her ongoing grief for the loss of Patrice.

Golden described her granddaughter as a dedicated mother and nurse.

Although the arrest brings some closure, there remain numerous unanswered questions the family seeks to address as they navigate the complex process of moving forward.
