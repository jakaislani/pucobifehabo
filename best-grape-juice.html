Best Grape Juice, Ranked [Taste Test]

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, September 6, 2024

The best grape juice is tart, refreshing, and sweet without being a total sugar bomb. We tried everything from concord to white, grape juice in gigantic plastic bottles and little dainty glass ones, to find the best grape juice you can buy.

Know what I like about grape juice? It’s a bev for people who like to live on the edge. Some people get a charge from shoplifting or having sex in public. Other people drink nice, tall glasses of grape juice in rooms with light-colored carpet—same basic idea.

For this taste test, we braved purple T-shirt stains to try more than a dozen brands of grape juice to find the best grape juice for people with a sense of adventure (or hardwood floors). Without further ado, these are the seven best grape juices you can buy.

Best of the Best

R.W. Knudsen Organic Concord GrapeInstacartWalmart

There’s a lot of big, bold grape flavor in this little glass bottle of organic grape juice. It’s tart and tannic without drying your mouth out, a problem common to grape juice. Like, why drink juice if it’s just going to make you more thirsty? Insane. Sporked editor-in-chief Justine Sterling described this as “grown-up tasting,” and that feels right. Grape juice isn’t just for sippy cups. This one’s good enough for a wine glass if you’re feeling fancy.

Credit: Merc / Instacart

Rating:

10/10

Best Drinkable

Santa Cruz Organic Concord GrapeInstacartKroger

I don’t think you can always tell the difference between organic and non-organic fruit juices, but all the best grape juice we tasted happened to be organic. If you want the best tasting grape juice, go organic! This Santa Cruz grape juice—which comes in a bottle that’s almost identical to R.W. Knudsen—is super smooth and highly drinkable. Like the Knudsen, it’s tart without drying your mouth out. I would guzzle this right out of the bottle, but it feels fancy enough to drink with dinner too.

Credit: Merc / Instacart

Rating:

9.5/10

Best Tart

Trader Joe’s Organic Concord Grape JuiceTraderjoes.com

Good grape juice shouldn’t be all sweetness—it should have dimension. If you like something with plenty of mouth-smacking tartness, this organic concord grape juice from Trader Joe’s is a good option. Sporked staff writer Danny Palumbo liked how this one sort of lingered on his tongue after he took a sip, and Justine liked its “extracted quality.” I get what she means. It’s like you can taste grape skins in the juice. This grape juice is complex and good.

Credit: Merc

Rating:

9/10

Sporks

Best Thirst Quencher

365 Organic Concord GrapeWhole FoodsAmazon

I tried a lot of these concord grape juices alongside Welch’s (see below) to get a feel for how their flavors compared to the flavor of a grape juice many of us are most familiar with. This grape juice from Whole Foods, for instance, was nice and sweet like Welch’s, but the sweetness was toned down just enough to make it highly drinkable. Serve this very chilled and it’s just so refreshing. It has a lighter body too, which makes it feel like it’s actually quenching your thirst rather than just giving you a sugar high.

Credit: Merc / Amazon

Rating:

9/10

Best Budget

Great Value GrapeWalmart

This Great Value grape juice comes in an enormous bottle and it tastes better than Welch’s. The best part is that it’s a generic brand, so it’s cheap to boot. We’re pretty consistently impressed with Great Value products, and this is another good’un. It’s a little more nuanced that Welch’s and seemed even fruitier and juicier. And, like I said, big-ass bottle!

Credit: Merc / Walmart

Rating:

8.5/10

Best Classic

Welch’s ConcordWalmartVons

Welch’s is the big name in grape juice. While it isn’t the best grape juice we tried, it was still good enough to secure a spot on the list. It has a nice, rich grape flavor, but it’s also super tannic and sort of saps your mouth of moisture. There are better grape juices out there, but we get why Welch’s is a classic.

Credit: Merc / Walmart

Rating:

8/10

Sporks

Best White Grape

Signature Select White Grape JuiceInstacartSafeway

After tasting several popular white grape juices, I came to the conclusion that white grape juice just does not taste as good as concord grape juice. White grapes are great for eating out of the bag, but less great for juicing. But I get it. Maybe you have kids and don’t want to spend your life cleaning purple stains out of things. Well, you don’t mind the much more subdued flavor, this big ol’ bottle from Signature Select is the best white grape juice you can buy—much better than Welch’s, in my opinion.

Credit: Merc / Instacart

7.5/10
