The 1987 Miss Carolina USA, entertainment journalist, Nancy O'Dell is living a single life at the mo

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, July 24, 2024

Nancy O'Dell is an American entertainment journalist who has served as co-anchor of the entertainment news show Entertainment Tonight. O'Dell is also identified as the weekday, correspondent, for the program Access Hollywood.

The media personality was born as Nancy Evelyn Humphries on February 25, 1966, in Sumter, South Carolina, USA. She has an elder sister named Karen Humphries.

Regarding education, Nancy attended Coastal Academy High School. Later, she earned her Bachelor's degree in marketing from Clemson University. 

Relationship Status

The journalist Nancy has not revealed her exact relationship status. She has not mentioned whether she is dating someone or is living a single life. 

It seems like the woman is enjoying her single life at the moment as she has not shared any romantic pictures on her official social accounts. After the tragic separation with former love partner's O'Dell has kept her relationship status within herself. Nancy is more focused on her career at the moment.

Was Married To Dr. Richard O'Dell

The 54-years-old Nancy was previously married to Dr. Richard O'Dell. Dr. Richard is an Anesthesiologist who received a Doctorate of Medicine from the University of Virginia.

Richard and Nancy tied the wedding knot on September 30, 1995. The former husband-wife duo was living merrily. Everything was going fine; however, after 10-years the couple divorced on November 4, 2004.

Nancy's Second Husband

After the tragic separation with Richard, Nancy started her romantic relationship with Keith Zubulevich. Keith is the chief strategy officer at Conviva.

After several months of dating, Keith and Nancy married on June 29, 2005. The intimate wedding ceremony held at Bacara Resort in Santa Barbara in front of their dearest ones. From the wedding day, the husband-wife duo was living merrily.

They also shared a daughter Ashby Grace Zubchevich in June 2007. Not only Ashby, but Nancy was also parenting her step-kids Tyler and Carson Zubchevich. The married pair (Keith & Nancy) were sharing a good bond. But citing some irreconcilable differences in November 2017 they partitioned their relationship.

Net Worth: $16 million

The journalist Nancy has a net worth of $16 million. She collected these hefty sums of wealth from her journalism career and from several other professional works.

O'Dell served as the anchor for Access Hollywood for 13-years from 1996 to 2006. She has also contributed to the Television shows like Dateline NBC and Today. Similarly, from 2011 to 2019 she served as a special correspondent for Entertainment Tonight (ET). 

As per the sources, Nancy's salary during her time on ET was around $4 million. She has also hosted the functions like Nashville Star, NBC's 65th Annual Golden Globe Awards, Miss Universe 2006 pageant, Miss USA 2007 pageant, Miss USA 2004, and many others. She is also the winner of 1987 Miss South Carolina.

Furthermore, O'Dell has also authored the book titled Stopping Hyperactivity: A New Solution, Stopping ADD/ADHD, and Learning Disabilities, and Full of Life: Mom-to-Mom Tips I Wish Someone Had Told Me When I Was Pregnant.

Movie Appearance

The host Nancy has also portrayed a minor role in the movie and TV shows such as Running Inside Out, The Adventures of Ragtime, The Bachelor, Martin Lawrence Live: Runteldat, Charmed, Scream 2, Scream 3, etc. 

The 1997 slasher film Scream 2 collected $172.4 million in its box-office collection under the production budget of $24 million where Nancy has played a minor character as a reporter. For the information, actress Jada Pinkett Smith has also performed in Scream 2.
