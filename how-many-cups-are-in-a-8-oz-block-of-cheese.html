Is 8 oz of shredded cheese the same as 8 oz of block cheese?

The contents of an 8 oz bag of shredded cheese and an 8 oz block of cheese is the same. It’s the weight of actual cheese in the bag vs block that’s different. Minus the added agents in a bag of shredded. Most shredded cheese sold in a bag has added agents to preserve and/or to inhibit cheese from clumping.
