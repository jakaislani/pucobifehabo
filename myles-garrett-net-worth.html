Myles Garrett | Career

The NFL franchise Cleveland Browns picked up Myles Garrett as the first pick in the first round of the NFL draft 2017.

Nevertheless, Myles Garrett made his NFL debut against the New York Jets on October 8, 2017.

Myles Garrett finished his rookie NFL year with total tackles of 28, including 16 solos, seven sacks, and a fumble recovered in 11 out of 16 games.

Moreover, Garrett’s astonishing performances in 2018 NFL season earned a first ever Pro Bowl.
