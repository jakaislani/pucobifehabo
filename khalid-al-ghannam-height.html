Khalid Al-Ghannam Height: How Tall is Khalid Al-Ghannam?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, May 22, 2024

Khalid Al-Ghannam Height: How Tall is Khalid Al-Ghannam? – Khalid Al-Ghannam is a highly talented Saudi Arabian professional footballer renowned for his role as a winger in the Saudi Pro League, where he currently contributes to the success of Al-Nassr.

Born on 7 November 2000, Al-Ghannam’s journey in professional football took root in the youth teams of Al-Qadsiah. His commitment and skill were quickly recognized, leading to the signing of his inaugural professional contract with the club on 22 January 2019. His debut with the first team transpired on 19 April 2019, an experience etched in memory despite the 2–0 loss against Al-Batin.

Standing at an impressive height of 1.71 meters, he possesses a commanding physical presence that complements his endeavors in various spheres of life. This dimension not only contributes to his stature but also serves as a distinctive aspect of his overall persona.

Demonstrating his dedication to Al-Qadsiah, Al-Ghannam extended his contract with the club on 19 August 2019, securing his presence until 2024. During his time with Al-Qadsiah, he exhibited his prowess in 16 appearances across various competitions, notably scoring a goal against Al-Bukayriyah.

In a significant move in his career, on 29 January 2020, Al-Ghannam bid farewell to his boyhood club and embraced a new challenge by signing a remarkable 5-year contract with Pro League champions, Al-Nassr. The transfer, valued at SAR 20 million, also saw Faraj Al-Ghushayan join Al-Qadsiah on loan, highlighting the strategic nature of the deal.

Continuing to evolve as a player, Al-Ghannam embarked on a temporary stint with Al-Fateh on 28 January 2023, committing to a six-month loan. This move represents a strategic decision in his career, providing him with new opportunities and challenges to further refine his skills on the field. As Al-Ghannam continues to make waves in the football scene, his journey promises excitement and success on both personal and team fronts.
