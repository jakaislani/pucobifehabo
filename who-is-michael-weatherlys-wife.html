Who is Michael Weatherly's wife?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, September 16, 2024

MICHAEL Weatherly is an actor and producer who is best known for playing Anthony DiNozzo in the television series NCIS.

He has been married to his wife for a number of years.

Who is Michael Weatherly's wife?

Bojana Jankovic is an award-winning doctor from Serbia.

She is a fellow of the American College of Physicians and double board-certified in internal and integrative medicine. 

Bojana is trained in functional medicine, nutrition, and mindfulness according to her website.

Patients typically come to Dr Bojana for "their gut health, thyroid health, energy levels and anxiety among many other conditions."

She was recognized as the Southern California Top Doctors’ Rising Star in 2016 and 2017 and awarded the Top Doctor recognition in 2018 – 2021 in New York.

She completed medical school, Master of Science (Experimental Medicine), and Bachelor of Science (Biophysics Honors) degrees at the University of British Columbia in Vancouver, Canada.

How long have they been married?

Michael and Bojana got hitched in 2009 after meeting at a bar in Vancouver in 2017.

Michael was previously married to actress Amelia Heinle between 1995 and 1997.

In 2017, Michael referred to his first marriage as a "difficult time."

“When I got remarried, I wanted to do my very best to ensure that rupture wouldn’t happen again,” he told PEOPLE. “My kids and my marriage are the things I’m proudest of.”

Do they have any children?

The pair have two children together: daughter Olivia and son Liam.

Michael also shares son August, 25, with ex-wife Amelia.

In the same 2017 interview with PEOPLE, Michael stated that he always makes family a top priority in his schedule.

“I don’t bring my work home,” Michael said. “I’m a single-track-mind person, so at work I do my work, but at home I’m devoted to my kids.”

“Acting has given me a way of understanding myself,” he said of his personal growth. “It’s made me a better person.”
