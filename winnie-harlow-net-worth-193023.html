About

Born Chantelle Brown-Young and also known as Chantelle Winnie, she is a Canadian model and spokesperson who competed on the 21st season of The CW's America's Next Top Model.

Before Fame

She was asked by Shannon Boodram to be a part of Shannon's YouTube music video "Natural Me" in 2013, opening the door for her career in modeling.

Trivia

She has been afflicted with the skin condition vitiligo since the age of four. She was bullied as a child because of the disease, leading her to dropping out of high school and having suicidal thoughts. She would finally come to peace with her appearance and instead use it to inspire others.

Family Life

She and sister Christina Harlow were born to parents Windsor Young and Lisa Brown-Young in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. She is of Jamaican descent.

Associated With

She was featured in the music video for Eminem's song "Guts Over Fear."
