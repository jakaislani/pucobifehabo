Kevin Love Biography

Kevin Love (full name: Kevin Wesley Love) is an American professional basketball player, playing for the Cleveland Cavaliers of the National Basketball Association (NBA). He is a five-time All-Star and in 2016 he won an NBA championship with the Cavaliers. His middle name, Wesley, is in honor of Wes Unseld, the former Washington Bullets center and the Loves’ family friend.

Love played high school basketball for the Lake Oswego Lakers. Playing for Lake Oswego High School, he averaged 25.3 points and 15.4 boards as a sophomore, 28.0 and 16.1 as a junior and 33.9 and 17.0 as a senior – leading his team to three straight state title games.

In July 2006, Love verbally committed to play college basketball at UCLA. As a collegian at UCLA and in his six years with the Timberwolves before being traded to Cleveland, Love’s numbers are numbing. Last season alone, he became the first player in NBA history to total at least 2,000 points, 900 boards and 100 made threes. He led the league in double-doubles (65), defensive rebounding (9.6) and amount of games (24) in which he registered at least 20 points, 10 boards and five assists

Years ago, he recorded 53 straight double-doubles and over the course of his half-dozen years in the Twin Cities, the Santa Monica native has averaged 19.2 points, 12.2 boards and 2.5 assists per contest.

Kevin Love Age

How old is Kevin Love? The Cleveland Cavaliers star was born on September 7, 1988 in Santa Monica, California, United States. He is 30 years old as of 2018.

Kevin Love Family

Kevin was born in Santa Monica into the family of Karen and Stan Love. He grew up in Lake Oswego, Oregon and is the second born in a family of three. His younger sibling is his sister by the name Emily, while his older sibling is his brother, Collin.
