Who is Timothy Nga wife?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, May 7, 2024

Timothy Nga has been confirmed dead on the 9th day of January 2023 leading to sad reactions from many people around the world who are fans of Singapore Local movies. 

But then how well do you know the actor? Do you know much about his family? What about Timothy Nga Wife ? This article will exclusively bring you every detail information you should know about the late Actor. 

Who is Timothy Nga? 

Just like other countries, Movies has played a large role in terms of entertaining the masses out there, it is same in Singapore and Timothy Nga is one of the leading icons in the Singapore Movie industry. 

Timothy Nga Movies

The late Timothy played roles in high profile local Singapore series such as Serangoon Road (2013), Fighting Spiders (2009), First Class (2009) and Lifeline (2005). Before his death, His most recent TV role was as Guan Meng in This Land Is Mine back in the year 2021.

Timothy Nga Wife

Timothy Nga does not have a wife, Yes in fact the late Actor was not married to anyone according to public knowledge. He constantly shared details about his professional life, hobbies, and family through his social media accounts but there has not been a single post from him that hints about his love and marital life. 

Many Superstars like Timothy sometimes love to keep their Relationship or marriage life away from the media, but background checks also indicates there has been no trace of him having a marital affair with any woman.

Timothy Nga Death

Timothy Nga’s sister Rina Nga  confirmed the death of her brother at the age of 49. In an Instagram post she wrote “My handsome brother, 10/6/73 — 9/1/23. Too soon, too young. Lived and loved life beyond its fullness.

“Remember we once planned a fantastic funeral together for Daddy when he passed on. Never thought I’d be planning yours. You even managed to get your minions (all five) running around and taking care of everything. I miss you, boy.”.
