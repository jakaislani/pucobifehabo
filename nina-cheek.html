Nina Cheek is the rising star in Hollywood. The multi-skilled girl is good at not just acting, yet dancing and singing as well. She has been winning the hearts of millions of fans through her acting prowess on the most-watched show titled “13 Reasons Why” featuring Katherine Langford. It was the top-ranked Netflix series watched by millions of people across the globe. And that became the reason for the young, charming actress.

She was born on 14th May 1998, California, USA where she raised and grew up with her brother and parents. She is mainly loved for her appealing looks, charming smile, her dark brown eyes, and blonde hair that adds glam to her personality.

She studied at Santa Rosa Junior College, California, USA and besides studies, she was so passionate about learning various dance forms and singing styles. So, finally, she did a specialization in such fields to hone her gifted skills.

Career

Nina Cheek has also worked in various shows including “With Kids” which was aired in 2017. In 2018, she appeared in the show named “Bumblebee” and also appeared in “Budding Projects” in 2017.

Before joining her professional career, she was a great singer and dancer who got specialization in hip-hop and modern forms of dance.

She made her acting debut with the film named “Budding Projects” which was the best release of the year 2017. It was the show that helped her to rise into fame and became extremely popular among her fans and followers across the globe.

Nina Cheek is too young and this is the perfect time to make her own identity in the cinematic world so presently, she is just focusing on her career.

Achievement

She earned success after playing the role in the film named “Budding Projects” which was released in 2017. She calls it one of the major achievements that incredibly, she has won the millions of hearts with her stellar performance.
