NFLs wide receiver Randall Cobbs wedding details with his longtime girlfriend turned wife, Aiy

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, June 9, 2024
  • Randall Cobb is an African-born football wide receiver who plays for the Green Bay Packers of the National Football League in the USA.
  • The sportsman has found his lady love and tied the knot with her in a lavish ceremony.

Let’s know all the details of his wedding with his partner.

The Marriage

The couple, Randall Cobb and his longtime girlfriend Aiyda Ghahramani were engaged and announced their wedding date on 15 April 2017. They decided to vow in front of their 250 guests in New York.

He says,

“I’m excited to have the people we love most, together, in the same room to celebrate our union. I’m very lucky to marry my best friend and spend the rest of my life with her.”

Aiyda commented,

“I can’t wait to share my life with Randall. He is truly the kindest, most supportive gentleman I have ever met.”

She also added that they are first best friends and later they are lovers,

“Our foundation of love has always been our friendship. We are truly best friends, partners and equals.”

Randall’s teammates, Aaron Rodgers and Jordy Nelson were present at his wedding.

How did they meet?

Randal and Aiyda met for the first time in the year 2011. They met in New York, a week before Randall’s NFL draft. During that time he was playing for Green Bay Packers and she was in the Law school in New Hampshire.

It took them three years to understand that they are made for each other and are ready to live together.

She said during that time,

“We both realized the timing was not right to start something romantic. But we became inseparable.”

She added,

“Our friendship will always be the foundation of our love. We just have such a comfortable, easy relationship… While I was focused on studying for the bar exam, Randall made every effort to be my biggest support system. He would make me breakfast every morning, take me to work, pick me up, make dinner, and sit in silence while I studied.”

The romantic proposal

Randall’s proposal to his lady love was no less than a fairytale. The day was her birthday and he took Aiyda where they spoke for the first time with each other.

Also, he decorated the path with flowers and candles,

“Randall walked me through a path of candles to the exact spot where he first said hello. I couldn’t believe that he remembered.”

He asked her whether she wanted to spend the rest of her life where they first spoke to each other and she replied, YES!’ And the rest is history.

The couple is living a very blissful life without any rumors or news of divorce or an extra-marital affair.
